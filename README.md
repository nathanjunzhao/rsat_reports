# rsat_reports
A link to all Rice Sports Analytics Reports for Rice University's 2023-2024 NCAA Divison I Women's Basketball Season.

This document contains all of the reports made the Rice Sports Analytics Team for the Women's Basketball Team at Rice University. 
As a Data Analyst on the RSAT, I contributed to all of these reports on varying aspects on a weekly basis throughout the NCAA Divison I Women's basketball season. 
The report contains aggregated statistics, advanced metrics, visualizations, and communicable directives for taking advantage of these reports.
